Short and Long Term Rentals for Multitouch Tables

Rent an Innovatie Touch Table

Ideum's high-tech Platform 46 and 32 multitouch tables and 4K UHD Platform 55 Drafting Multitouch Table can add an engaging element to your next event.

For the first time, these original touch tables are available for short and long term rentals. These unique interactive multitouch tables are perfect for trade shows, special events, traveling exhibits, temporary installations, and broadcast. Ideum is known for its quality builds, iconic designs and push-button operation. Ideum's touch tables have appeared in cutting-edge technology blogs such as Engadget, Gizmag, Slashgear, Discovery News, PC Magazine, and many others.

Custom built in the US, Ideum sells (and now rents!) these tables directly to museums, universities, research labs, retail spaces, and Fortune 500 companies. The tables are designed specifically for public spaces. Set up is simple, the tables do not require a technician or assembly, they come in a wheeled ATA travel case, ready to plug in and turn on.

The Platform 46 and 32 multitouch tables use a non-optical touch technology developed by 3M and can work in any lighting environment. The tables are water resistant (IP54 rating) and use the same touch technology found in tablets and smart phones. They are bezel-free and can work in demanding environments such as restaurants, cafes, bars, and nightclubs, so they are well equipped for special events and tradeshows.

Platform 46 Full-Sized Table

The Platform 46 tables combine Ideum's design and build-quality with a 3M™ high-performance multitouch display to present a rugged turnkey multitouch table built for public environments. Along with its innovative design, the Platform 46 comes with an integrated and powerful desktop Intel i7 and is the only touch table to feature the ultra-fast and precise 3M™ Multitouch Display (C4667PW) with support for 60 touch points.

Platform 46 Drafting Multitouch Table

The Platform 46 Drafting Multitouch Table is a fully integrated system incorporating a large multitouch screen set at a 30-degree angle. It features the 3M™ Multi-Touch Display C4667PW projected capacitive touch screen. The 46' bezel-less, ultra-fast display supports 60 simultaneous touch points and comes complete with an integrated Intel Quad-Core i7 with 8GB RAM. It has lockable access points, single push-button power and comes ready to plug in and use.

Learn more on the Platform Drafting Multitouch Table website

4K UHD Platform 55 Drafting Multitouch Table

The 4K UHD Platform 55 Drafting Multitouch Table is integrated with a powerful 4K compatible computer; it's display is set at a fixed 30 degree angle and is capable of up to 40 simultaneous touch points. Outfitted for the performance needed to support eye-popping ultra high definition and multiuser interaction, the 4K Platform 55 Drafting Multitouch Table is right at home in architectural and construction settings, museums, trade shows, retail, and other public spaces.
