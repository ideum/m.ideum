Platform Coffee Table

Platform 46 Coffee Table

The Platform 46 Coffee Table, featuring the 3M™ Multi-Touch Display C4667PW is a complete turnkey multitouch table that comes with Windows 8 (and soon Android OS). It has a bezel-less, 46” multitouch screen with support for 60 touch points.

Platform 32 Coffee Table

The Platform 32 Coffee Table features a bezel-less 3M™ Multi-Touch Display, a ruggedized and secure build, and is a complete, turnkey multitouch table with support for 40 touch points. The touch table ships with Windows 8 (and soon Android OS).

Platform

Platform

The Platform is a complete ruggedized 55” multitouch table that comes with a powerful integrated PC with an i7 quad core, built-in RFID, support for up to 40 touch points, and is available with either an Active 3D LED LCD HD or 4k Ultra HD display.

Platform 46

The Platform 46, featuring the 3M™ Multi-Touch Display C4667PW, is a complete ruggedized 46” projected capacitive multitouch table that is equipped with a powerful integrated PC, single push-button power, and support for 60 touch points.

Platform Drafting Table

Platform 46 Drafting Table

The Platform 46 Drafting Multitouch Table, featuring the 3M ™ Multi-Touch Display C4667PW, is a complete ruggedized 46” projected capacitive multitouch table. It features an angled display and is equipped with a powerful integrated PC, single push-button power, and support for 60 touch points.

Platform 55/65 Drafting Table

The Platform 55/65 Drafting Multitouch Tables are a complete ruggedized touch table available in 55” and 65”. They feature an Active 3D LED LCD HD or 4k Ultra HD angled display and are equipped with a powerful integrated PC, single push-button power, and support for up to 40 touch points.

Pro

Pro

The Pro is a 55” multitouch table with a powerful workstation, HD resolution, up to 40 touch points, an integrated UPS, Yamaha audio system, and is available with either an Active 3D LED LCD HD or 4k Ultra HD display.

Pro Lab

The Pro Lab Build is a 55” multitouch table with a powerful workstation, HD resolution, up to 40 touch points, expandable and removable storage (up to 6 TB), an integrated UPS, Yamaha audio system, and is available with either an Active 3D LED LCD HD or 4k Ultra HD display.

Pano

Pano

The Pano is a hardened, integrated 100” multitouch table with dual 55” Active 3D LED LCD HD or 4k Ultra HD displays and a contiguous touch surface with 40+ touch points.
